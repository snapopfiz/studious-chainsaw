MRSE v2

Modern Relative Stopping Effectiveness Calculator

Welcome

MRSE v2 is a practical, modern stopping power formula designed for today's ammunition. It improves on classic systems like Hatcher RSP by including expansion, penetration, and shot placement.

The Formula

MRSE = (W × V × A_exp × E_f / 7000) × P_f × R × PM × M_f

Variables:

  • W = Bullet weight (grains)
  • V = Velocity (fps)
  • A_exp = Expanded frontal area (π × (D_exp/2)²)
  • E_f = Energy transfer factor (V/1000)
  • P_f = Penetration factor (ideal 12–18" in gel)
  • R = Reliability (0.7–1.1)
  • PM = Placement multiplier (Brain 2.5 → Extremity 0.3)
  • M_f = Multiple-hit factor (2 shots = 1.6)

How It Works

This formula rewards modern expanding ammunition that meets FBI gel standards (12–18" penetration) while heavily weighting shot placement — the single biggest factor in real-world shootings.

It is a heuristic tool, not a guarantee. Real stopping power depends on shot placement, physiology, and attacker determination.
